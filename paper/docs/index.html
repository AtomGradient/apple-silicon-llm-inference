Key Findings 核心发现

Six results from systematic benchmarking across three Apple Silicon machines. 覆盖三台 Apple Silicon 设备的系统性基准测试揭示的六项关键发现。

⚡

Speed Ratio > Acceptance Rate 速比比接受率更重要

On Apple Silicon, SD benefit is governed by draft/target speed ratio. A 3.3× ratio yields +25.7% speedup even at 2–4% acceptance rate, due to Metal GPU batch-verification efficiency. 在 Apple Silicon 上，推测采样（SD）的收益由 draft/target 速比决定。3.3× 速比在仅 2–4% 接受率下仍能实现 +25.7% 提速，得益于 Metal GPU 批处理验证效率。

📈

Q6_K is Pareto-Optimal Q6_K 是 Pareto 最优点

Q8_0 is near-lossless (0.18% PPL). Q6_K dominates Q5_K_M on both speed and quality. Q4_K_M is the memory-constrained sweet spot. Sub-4-bit is unusable (Q2_K: +267% PPL). Q8_0 近乎无损（0.18% PPL 损失）。Q6_K 在速度和质量上均优于 Q5_K_M。Q4_K_M 是内存受限场景的推荐选择。4bit 以下实际不可用（Q2_K：+267% PPL）。

❌

Self-Speculation Fails 自推测采样不可行

Same-model-different-quant SD (4B Q2→Q8) achieves only 1.35× speed ratio, causing ~25% throughput loss. Requires ≥2.5× to break even on unified memory. 同模型不同量化的 SD（4B Q2→Q8）速比仅 1.35×，导致约 25% 吞吐量下降。统一内存架构下需 ≥2.5× 速比才能收支平衡。

🆕

MoE + SD Conflict MoE 与 SD 存在冲突

Qwen3.5-35B-A3B MoE achieves 55.2 tok/s (only ~3B active params). Its sparse expert routing makes draft predictions unreliable; SD offers no benefit. Qwen3.5-35B-A3B MoE 达到 55.2 tok/s（每 token 仅激活 ~3B 参数）。稀疏 expert routing 使 draft 预测不可靠；SD 无益。

💾

QAT Shows Smooth Gradients QAT 呈现平滑梯度

Gemma-3-4B QAT spans 96→137 tok/s (3→8 bit) with σ < 0.3 tok/s. PTQ Qwen3-8B shows σ≈19 tok/s variance at low bit-widths. Gemma-3-4B QAT 从 8bit 到 3bit 吞吐从 96→137 tok/s，σ < 0.3 tok/s。PTQ Qwen3-8B 低精度下 σ≈19 tok/s 方差较大。

⚠

Cross-Device SD: GGML_RPC Not Viable 跨设备 SD：GGML_RPC 不可用于生产

GGML_RPC cross-device SD incurs ~79–83% throughput reduction from per-op RPC protocol overhead (~51–57 ms/token), not network bandwidth. Initial BLAS-build results (−2%) were invalid due to silent local fallback. Purpose-built streaming protocol needed. GGML_RPC 跨设备 SD 因逐 op RPC 协议开销（约 51–57 ms/token）导致 ~79–83% 吞吐下降，而非网络带宽瓶颈。最初的 BLAS 版结果（−2%）因本地静默回退而无效。需要专用流式协议。
